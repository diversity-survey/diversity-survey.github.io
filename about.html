About the Diversity Survey

Our mission is to gather valuable insights on diversity and inclusion to foster a more equitable and inclusive environment.

Objectives

  • Understand the current state of diversity.
  • Identify areas for improvement.
  • Support policies and initiatives that promote inclusion.

How It Works

The survey is anonymous and confidential. It will only take a few minutes to complete and your response will help to create a dataset that can be analyzed to come to conclusions and help shape our efforts to bring change.
